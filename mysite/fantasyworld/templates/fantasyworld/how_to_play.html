The Street League

How Do I Play?

Have you ever wanted to try to outwager your friends (or public experts) without contributing to their financial woes? Here's your chance.


Step 1: join a league -- either a public league open to everyone, or a private league with friends.


Step 2: start placing bets. Think the Falcons will win 10 games instead of 7 this year? Buy their season win total for only $7, and get a $1 payout for each game they actually win. Think they've become overrated when they start 4-0, and we're pricing them to win 9 more games the rest of the season? Sell the bet off. You'll end up with $13 -- $4 for each game they won, and $9 for their rest of season price.


It's that simple. Grow your bankroll, and whoever ends up with the most money at the end of the season wins. Or, to really test your skills, join a freeplay league and bet on any lines we have available in the site -- politics, sports, or more as we develop them -- and see how high you can grow your bankroll
